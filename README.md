# 
Privacy Policy – Game Library Tracker

Last updated: 2025-12-01

Thank you for using Game Library Tracker.
This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect
1.1 Personal Information

We do not collect or store personally identifiable information such as your name, email address, phone number, or contact details.

The app allows you to sign in with your Steam account only to access your game library.

We do not store your Steam password.

Authentication is handled securely via Steam’s official systems.

Steam profile data is used only to display your game library and activity inside the app.

1.2 Steam Game Data

After signing in with Steam, the app may access:

Your owned games

Playtime information

Game metadata (title, cover, platform)

This data is used only to let you:

Rate games

Write personal notes or reviews

Track your game status (played, completed, backlog, etc.)

This data is not shared with third parties.

1.3 Usage Data

We may collect anonymous usage data to improve the app, such as:

Device type and operating system

App version

Feature usage (e.g. rating games, adding notes)

Crash reports and performance logs

This data does not identify you personally.

2. Payments & Monetization

Game Library Tracker is a paid application.

There are no advertisements, no subscriptions, and no in-app purchases.

Payment is handled entirely by Apple App Store / Google Play Store.

We do not receive or store any payment or billing information.

3. How We Use Information

We use collected data only to:

Display your Steam game library

Improve app performance and stability

Fix bugs and crashes

Enhance user experience

We do not sell, rent, or share your data with advertisers.

4. Data Storage & Security

Game ratings, notes, and statuses are stored securely.

Steam-related data is accessed only when needed.

Industry-standard security practices are used to protect all data.

5. Third-Party Services

The app may use:

Steam API for game library access

Analytics tools (e.g. Firebase, Sentry) for crash and performance monitoring

Apple / Google services for app distribution and payment

Each third-party service operates under its own privacy policy.

6. Children’s Privacy

Game Library Tracker is not intended for children under the age of 13.

7. Your Rights

You may request:

Deletion of locally stored app data

Information about collected data

Assistance regarding privacy concerns

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time.
Changes will be reflected with a new “Last updated” date.
