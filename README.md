# 
Privacy Policy – White Noise AI

Last updated: 2025-12-01

Thank you for using White Noise AI.
This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, protect, and handle your data when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Personal Information
We do not collect any personally identifiable information such as your name, email address, phone number, or contact data.

1.2 Usage Data
We may collect anonymous usage information to improve the app, such as:
- Device type and operating system
- App version
- Interaction events (e.g., which sounds are played, session durations)
- Crash logs and performance analytics
This data does not identify you personally.

1.3 AI Sound Generation Data
When you use the AI Sound Generator:
- The text prompts or settings you enter may be processed to generate audio.
- These prompts are not stored permanently and are not linked to your identity.
- Generated sounds may be cached locally on your device.

1.4 Subscription & In-App Purchases
Paid features use Apple/Google in‑app purchases. We do not receive or store any payment data.

2. How We Use Your Information
We use anonymized data to:
- Improve performance
- Enhance AI generation quality
- Understand feature usage
- Fix bugs and crashes

We do not sell or share data with advertisers.

3. Data Storage & Security
- AI-generated audio stays on your device unless you delete it.
- Anonymous analytics data is stored securely.
- Industry‑standard security practices are used.

4. Third‑Party Services
The App may use:
- AI model providers
- Analytics tools such as Firebase or Sentry
- Apple/Google billing services

Each provider maintains its own privacy policy.

5. Children’s Privacy
The App is not intended for children under 13.

6. Your Rights
You may request:
- Data deletion
- Information regarding collected data
- Opt‑out of analytics (where supported)

Contact us to exercise your rights.

7. Changes to This Policy
We may update this Privacy Policy periodically.
